# BattleCard Arena - Product Overview

## Product Purpose
A gamified trading card battle arena where users create, collect, and battle with digital trading cards. Educational paper trading simulation with no real money involved.

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Gamers interested in trading card mechanics (ages 16-35)
- **Secondary**: Developers learning web3/trading concepts
- **Tertiary**: Trading card enthusiasts exploring digital formats

## Key Features
- **Card Creation**: Design custom battle cards with stats and abilities
- **Battle System**: Turn-based combat using card mechanics
- **Collection Management**: Organize and trade cards with other players
- **Leaderboards**: Competitive rankings and achievements
- **Paper Trading**: Educational simulation environment only

## Business Objectives
- Demonstrate modern web development patterns
- Showcase AI-assisted development workflow
- Create engaging user experience with zero financial risk
- Build hackathon-winning application

## User Journey
1. **Onboarding**: View disclaimers, understand paper trading nature
2. **Card Creation**: Design first battle card with guided flow
3. **Collection**: Browse and organize card collection
4. **Battle**: Engage in turn-based battles with other players
5. **Progress**: Track wins, losses, and achievements

## Success Criteria
- Users can create and battle with cards within 5 minutes
- Zero crashes during demo scenarios
- Clear educational disclaimers prevent confusion
- Responsive design works on mobile and desktop
- Hackathon judges can run locally without external dependencies
